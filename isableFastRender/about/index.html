About

August 1, 2020 · 1 min

Computers have always played a pivotal role in my life. We had one when I was only five, and I started to pick up on them at the age of ten. Back then, all I wanted was to play around with the display buffer in DOS to depict something on the screen. Therefore, after a while, I made my first game called Snake! Since then, I have become a self-thought machine, so that is why I’m quite comfortable with learning new concepts.

My main passion is to create things and give life to them. Thereby, developing games along with the systems they are built on, is what I do for the most part.

In hindsight, as a computer engineer, I have worked on many projects using a broad range of technologies. Some of the more discernible ones are manifested on this page. Besides, my expertise must be listed somewhere around here, so be my guest; just do not break anything!
