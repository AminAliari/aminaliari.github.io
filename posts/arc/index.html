My journey at ARC

October 2, 2020 · 1 min

While I was a researcher at Amirkabir University of Technology Robotic Center, I’ve developed a simulation environment using Unity3D game engine to connect to ROS framework. This tool was mostly used to test our AI, movement, localization, and vision systems.

  • We needed this environment because other teams such as mechanic or electronic team would want to take robots to work on them and at the same time software team needed to continue their development plans.
  • This environment could even support having several clients (several robots) connecting to it and play in the same environment.
  • We used this environment on Windows and Mac too. because only the framework needed to be ran on Ubuntu.

I implemented a middleware to connect Unity3D to ROS framework over the network. I did some Socket Programming for that.
